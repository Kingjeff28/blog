My journey to becoming a web designer.

A few weeks ago, I was sitting at home thinking of ways that I can learn new skills without having to pay lots of money in tuition fees and without having to worry about being in debt for the rest of my life. While surfing the web I stumbled across an ad that said "Start your own business." As I began to read the ad in detail, the wheels in my head began to turn. I began to look at the world differently. I realized that everything in this world is becoming more and more technology driven. I realized that if I wanted to be successful, I mean really successful, then I should keep up with the current trend. Everyone is buying the latest tech gadgets. Or the latest tech savvy phone or car. There are millions of people around the world whose daily routine consists of using technology for more than half their day. More and more people are starting to invest their money into tech companies. So I asked myself, "Jeff, whats stopping you from developing then next big app?" "What's stopping you from owning your own tech company?" NOTHING! The only thing that can stop me is me. And I can't, won't and ain't going to let that happen. My total focus is centered around becoming the greatest web developers to ever live.

Shortly after making up my mind anout becoming a web developer, my wife found an app called MIM0. This app is amazing! This app helped a novice like me establish the fundimentals of web developing.
